## 🎯 Points System (Quick)

| Checkpoint Type | Max Points | Time Deduction |
|----------------|------------|----------------|
| Tracing (T1-T3) | 200 | ≤2min: 200pts<br>2-5min: -10/min<br>>5min: -5/min |
| Activity (T4-T7) | 200 | Same as tracing |
| Coding (C1-C2) | 300 | Same as tracing<br>Deferred: -20% |
| Final (FC) | 400 | No deduction |

**Maximum Total**: 2,400 points

---

## 📊 Game Rules (Quick)

- ✅ 10 checkpoints per team
- ✅ 3 tracing + 4 activity + 2 coding + 1 final
- ✅ No consecutive tracing rounds
- ✅ Coding never at positions 1 or 9
- ✅ 3 swaps per team (max 1 per checkpoint)
- ✅ Final locked until first 9 completed
